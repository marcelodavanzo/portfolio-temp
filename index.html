Hey there,

I'm Marcelo Davanzo, a digital product designer specialised in highly engaging experiences. I love solving complex problems, working alongside inspiring, creative people while keeping the user at the core of everything I do.

I've worked with brands such as P&G, ABC, Disney, Warner, Ford, Lacoste, Nintendo, NBC, Unilever, MailOnline, DailyMail.com and more, helping them create amazing digital experiences and products for their teams and customers.

I love solving complex problems, coming up with new ways of interacting with content and take pride in rigorously testing and refining user interfaces and ensuring experiences are top notch.

I believe usability goes hand in hand with design. Form follows function. There is no point in having a beautiful interface if your users can't create a true connection with it or get their work done.

When I'm not working, I like to play around on Codepen, get inspired on Dribbble, catch up on Medium, go to meetups and check out new apps and products and see what's out there and how people are doing things in the digital world.

TL;DR – I'm a digital product designer specialised in creating highly engaging experiences by combining my knowledge of web technologies, front-end development, UI, UX, interactive and visual design, and have delivered projects for various brands across multiple verticals.
